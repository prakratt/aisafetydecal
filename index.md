## AI Safety DeCal — Fall 2026 &nbsp;<span class="label label-yellow">Website under construction</span>

Welcome! The AI Safety DeCal provides a high-level understanding of the AI alignment problem and some of the key research directions which aim to solve it. Students will learn about the theoretical and practical risks associated with using advanced AI systems, the difficulties inherent to addressing them, and the current state of research regarding solutions.

**Location:** Dwinelle 88
<br>
**Time:** Wednesdays, 6–8 PM
<br>
**Units:** 1

**Instructors:**
[Prakrat Agrawal](mailto:prakrat@berkeley.edu),
[Ali Narin](mailto:ali_narin@berkeley.edu),
[Brandon Qi](mailto:bqi@berkeley.edu)
